Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 21, 2025

Create PDF: Scan and Sign App values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information and ensure your trust.

Data Collection

Create PDF: Scan and Sign App does not collect or transmit personal data. All actions — including scanning, editing, and saving documents — are processed locally on your device.

What This Means

  • No Uploads: Your documents remain on your device unless you choose to share them.
  • No Tracking: We do not track your activity or behavior in the app.
  • No Third Parties: We do not share any data with advertisers or analytics services.

Document Features

With Create PDF: Scann and Sign App, you can:

  • Scan physical documents using the camera
  • Import images from your gallery, iCloud, or other apps
  • Add text and your signature to pages
  • Save documents as PDFs
  • Share them quickly and securely

Children's Privacy

Create PDF: Scan and Sign App is suitable for all ages. It does not collect personal data from children or any other users.
